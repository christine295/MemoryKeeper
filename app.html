ℹ MemoryKeeper Help

Getting Started

MemoryKeeper helps you review, tag, and share your family photos. Your photos stay on your device — only resized previews are uploaded when you share.

Archives

Each archive is a named collection (e.g. "Grandma's Albums"). You can have multiple archives for different branches of your family.

Review Actions

  • ◆ Best Shot — print-quality photo worth preserving
  • ★ Favorite — personal favourite
  • ○ Hide — keeps but hides from share view
  • ✕ Delete (App Only) — removes from this app; file stays on disk
  • Save & Next — saves tags/notes and moves to next photo

Sharing

Click Share to upload resized previews of your photos and generate a link family members can open to add memories and names. Contributors don't need an account.

Reviewing Contributions

Click Contributions to review memory submissions and uploaded photos from family members. Accept a memory to merge it into your local metadata.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Arrow keys / Space — skip forward / backward
  • S — Save & Next
  • F — toggle Favorite
  • B — toggle Best Shot
  • H — toggle Hide
  • G — toggle Gallery view
  • Z — zoom current photo
  • ? — this help panel
  • Esc — close any panel

Data Storage

On Chrome/Edge: tags and notes are saved as mk-metadata.json directly in your photos folder — the file travels with your photos. On Firefox/Safari: data is kept in your browser's localStorage and can be exported as JSON.
